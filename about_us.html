Our Mission

At IKHTIYAAR, our mission is rooted in spreading smiles and fostering power and unity within our communities. We are dedicated to providing education and support to those in need, particularly focusing on children from orphanages and underprivileged backgrounds. Our goal is to empower these young minds with essential life knowledge, including education about the menstrual cycle, sex education, and resilience in the face of failure. Through our efforts, we aim to create a brighter future by equipping individuals with the tools they need to succeed and thrive.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to quality education and support. At IKHTIYAAR, we aspire to be a beacon of hope and opportunity, where generosity and compassion drive positive change. Through our ongoing drives and initiatives, we strive to provide basic necessities to the underprivileged and create a platform for meaningful contributions from our supporters. Together, we can build a better future by fostering education, empowerment, and unity within our communities.
