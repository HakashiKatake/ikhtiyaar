About Us

We are here for spreading smiles, IKHTIYAAR stands for power and unity, and we can proudly say that we are privileged enough to educate and help the ones in need. We aim to educate kids from the orphanage or the ones who can't afford it, educate them about basic life knowledge even about the menstrual cycle, sex education, how to deal with failures and much more. We also accept donations that will help us to complete our drives which will include providing basic necessities to the underprivileged, help us reach our goal by supporting us and if you can then kindly contribute with any small amount possible, let's hope for a better future?

Our Mission


At IKHTIYAAR, our mission is rooted in spreading smiles and fostering power and unity within our communities. We are dedicated to providing education and support to those in need, particularly focusing on children from orphanages and underprivileged backgrounds. Our goal is to empower these young minds with essential life knowledge, including education about the menstrual cycle, sex education, and resilience in the face of failure. Through our efforts, we aim to create a brighter future by equipping individuals with the tools they need to succeed and thrive.

Our Vision


We envision a world where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to quality education and support. At IKHTIYAAR, we aspire to be a beacon of hope and opportunity, where generosity and compassion drive positive change. Through our ongoing drives and initiatives, we strive to provide basic necessities to the underprivileged and create a platform for meaningful contributions from our supporters. Together, we can build a better future by fostering education, empowerment, and unity within our communities.

Our Works

Contribution Our Team Did!

CLEAN UP DRIVE

Ikhtiyaar strongly believes that keeping our nation clean and protecting and preserving the environment is one of the most important duty of every individual. Sticking to this belief, ikhtiyaar has done more that 20 cleanup drives collecting wastes from various places in Mumbai And Navimumbai. We have covered places like Taloja hill, Kharbhar Hill, Flamingo Point Seawoods, TS Chanakya, Prabhadevi Beach Mumbai etc. Few of the drives were collaborative which included organizations like Recycle and Mumbai Ploggers.

CLOTHES DRIVE

Ikhtiyaar once every 2-3 months conducts clothes distribution drives where it distributes 500-600 clothes to the needy people. Having done more than 4 clothes distribution drive, we have successfully helped 200+ plus people to have something to wear.

FOOD DRIVES

Hunger in one of the biggest problems that a poor has to suffer from. Keeping all other expenses aside, people don't have money to buy themselves a single course meal or to give their family good and filling food. This is one of the major reasons why majority of the underprivileged people suffer from malnutrition and starvation. Ikhtiyaar in order to address this problems conducts food distribution drives. One of the major food drives conducted by us was held on 3rd of September 2022 where more than 1000 food packets were distributed among the poor and needy people.

ORPHANAGE VISTS

ikhtiyaar with its volunteers regularly visits the orphanages and engages with kids. With more than 15+ drives, ikhtiyaar has bought smiles on the faces of many unfortunate kids by conducting workshops, engaging them in extracurricular activities, celebrating important days like Childrens Day , rakshabandhan etc. It has collaborated with ngos and orphanages win navimumbai which include Bethany Children's Home, Desire Society and Jeevan Jyoti Aashyalay

About The founders of Ikhtiyaar: Kanika Banerjee

Starting my own Ngo Ikhtiyaar introduced me to a new meaning of Unconditional Love. Visiting orphanages and knowing more about their lives made me realize that the statement blood is thicker than water is just a phrase on papers but reality can be different. A youth organization. The "GEN-Z" contributing towards a better future. Ikhtiyaar stands for Unity and power. We at Ikhtiyaar believe small actions shape and mold the present into a future of which we can all be proud of. Ikhtiyaar was founded on 3rd of June 2022 With a team of 60+ volunteers we strive for bringing a difference in the society by uplifting the underprivileged ones. We have successfully conducted 50+ drives since we started. We look forward to more drives and the youth of India joining hands with Ikhtiyaar for a Better future.

About The founders of Ikhtiyaar: Gauravi Dubey

"vasudhaiva kutumbakam" - The world in one family and so are we, I believe we all are connected through our souls just like how a family is connected through blood & the way we play many roles being in our family, we play several roles being a human being in this world & I am doing the same, Heading from Bihar, I have changed 9 schools and 9 cities (apparently my dad chose a government job) I ofc did not like leaving house, packing my life in a suitcase and then starting it all over again but slowly I have realised that my circumstances were all destined in order to make me the person I am today, at 15 | started my small business (teenage does give you a kick to become employed) and at 16 l started my own youth organisation which now has more than 200 members heading from all over India, my NPO Ikhtiyaar is my home and always makes me realise how we all are connected, I also believe in being a diplomat (wake up and smell the coffee) diplomacy is my key to a successful personal and professional life hence MUN's are what l have been doing lately to keep myself filled with current affairs and International Relations, Currently studying in 12th Standard with the aim of creating a better world for all
