Our Works

Contribution Our Team Did!

CLEAN UP DRIVE

Ikhtiyaar strongly believes that keeping our nation clean and protecting and preserving the environment is one of the most important duty of every individual. Sticking to this belief, ikhtiyaar has done more that 20 cleanup drives collecting wastes from various places in Mumbai And Navimumbai. We have covered places like Taloja hill, Kharbhar Hill, Flamingo Point Seawoods, TS Chanakya, Prabhadevi Beach Mumbai etc. Few of the drives were collaborative which included organizations like Recycle and Mumbai Ploggers.

CLOTHES DRIVE

Ikhtiyaar once every 2-3 months conducts clothes distribution drives where it distributes 500-600 clothes to the needy people. Having done more than 4 clothes distribution drive, we have successfully helped 200+ plus people to have something to wear.

FOOD DRIVES

Hunger in one of the biggest problems that a poor has to suffer from. Keeping all other expenses aside, people don't have money to buy themselves a single course meal or to give their family good and filling food. This is one of the major reasons why majority of the underprivileged people suffer from malnutrition and starvation. Ikhtiyaar in order to address this problems conducts food distribution drives. One of the major food drives conducted by us was held on 3rd of September 2022 where more than 1000 food packets were distributed among the poor and needy people.

ORPHANAGE VISTS

ikhtiyaar with its volunteers regularly visits the orphanages and engages with kids. With more than 15+ drives, ikhtiyaar has bought smiles on the faces of many unfortunate kids by conducting workshops, engaging them in extracurricular activities, celebrating important days like Childrens Day , rakshabandhan etc. It has collaborated with ngos and orphanages win navimumbai which include Bethany Children's Home, Desire Society and Jeevan Jyoti Aashyalay

About Us

We are here for spreading smiles, IKHTIYAAR stands for power and unity, and we can proudly say that we are privileged enough to educate and help the ones in need. We aim to educate kids from the orphanage or the ones who can't afford it, educate them about basic life knowledge even about the menstrual cycle, sex education, how to deal with failures and much more. We also accept donations that will help us to complete our drives which will include providing basic necessities to the underprivileged, help us reach our goal by supporting us and if you can then kindly contribute with any small amount possible, let's hope for a better future?
